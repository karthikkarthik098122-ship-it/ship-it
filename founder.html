About

My name is Karthik. I was born on April 2nd. I am from Paramakudi, Ramanathapuram. I completed my schooling at a higher secondary school in Paramakudi and pursued a Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) degree at Government Arts College. After completing my studies, I worked for about a year at a fraud company. During my time there, I worked as a co-founder, but unfortunately, I faced a major disappointment. I was not given what I deserved and was eventually let down by the company. That experience became a turning point in my life. Instead of giving up, I decided to take a bold step. I realized that many people like me had faced similar situations, so I wanted to create something better. I formed a team and met Edwin and Dinesh, who shared the same vision. Along with them, I decided to start a startup of our own. That is how Syro Tech Hub was born. One of the biggest supports in starting this journey was Vaishnavi, who had also worked at the same company and faced similar challenges. Among everyone, Vaishnavi played a very important role in helping me build Syro Tech Hub. Coming from a financially struggling background, I always had the dream of achieving something big in life. That struggle motivated me to start this company. My goal with Syro Tech Hub is to provide opportunities to talented individuals and support people who deserve a chance to grow. I also wanted to offer high-quality project services at a more affordable cost compared to other companies, making it accessible for everyone. My vision is to expand this startup across different districts and eventually take it to a global level by opening branches in multiple countries. Even today, I come across people from the same company that once disappointed me, but I no longer give them importance because I have moved forward with a bigger purpose. This is my life story and the journey behind Syro Tech Hub.
