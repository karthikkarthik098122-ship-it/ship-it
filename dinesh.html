About

Dinesh was born in Kanyakumari and comes from a well-settled family background. He completed his schooling in Kanyakumari and is currently pursuing his college education there, studying in his second year. He comes from a family where most members are working in IT-related jobs. Despite this, he initially struggled to prove himself, and there were times when he was not taken seriously at home. His brother, who has been working in a startup for over 15 years, often questioned him about his direction and progress. This pressure pushed Dinesh to take steps on his own to build something meaningful. During his early career phase, he worked on multiple projects through a fraud company. Despite putting in significant effort and delivering quality work, he was not paid for his projects and was ultimately deceived. His skills and hard work were taken advantage of, which became a major setback for him. After this experience, he met Karthik, Vaishnavi, and Edwin. Together, they shared their experiences and decided to build something of their own. This led to the creation of Syro Tech Hub. Motivated by the vision to grow bigger and avoid such negative experiences again, Dinesh joined the team and became an important part of the startup. He has contributed significantly to various areas and has been a strong support in building and developing the company. This is his journey and story.
