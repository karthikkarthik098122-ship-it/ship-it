About

Edwin Rakhul was born in Kerala, where he also completed his schooling in a government school. He pursued his college education in a government college and is currently in his third year. Coming from a financially challenging background, he was determined to build a better future for himself. In search of opportunities, he joined a fraud company, where he worked on multiple projects. Along with project work, he also served as a coach and trained around 40 students, showcasing his leadership and teaching abilities. During this time, he met Karthik, Vaishnavi, and Dinesh. All of them had faced similar experiences of being deceived by the same company. With the support of Vaishnavi and Dinesh, they formed a strong connection as a team. Realizing the reality of the fraud company, Edwin decided to leave and join hands with the team. Believing in their shared vision, he became one of the key creators of Syro Tech Hub. From the early stages, Edwin played an important role in the company’s growth. He actively contributed to collecting projects, onboarding members, and planning strategies to scale the company. He worked closely with the team to develop ideas on how to grow the business and achieve long-term success. This is his journey and contribution to Syro Tech Hub.
